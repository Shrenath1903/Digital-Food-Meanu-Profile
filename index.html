How Transparent Ordering Works

From scanning a QR code to enjoying confident, well-informed orders — a seamless, pressure-free dining journey.

01

Scan QR Code

Guests scan the table QR code directly into their phone browser — instant access, no app needed.

02

Explore With Details

Browse dishes with high-quality photos, ingredients, preparation method, and full descriptions — know exactly what you're ordering.

03

Discuss & Decide

Take your time with friends and family. Share the menu on-table, discuss preferences, and make confident choices without server pressure.

04

Order Stress-Free

Submit your order directly through the digital menu. AI system routes to kitchen — your meal arrives exactly as shown.
